Our Dental Treatments

We offer a comprehensive range of family and cosmetic dentistry services utilizing the latest digital diagnostic tools.

Preventative Care

Comprehensive checkups, gentle scaling, deep cleanings, and preventative care to maintain optimal oral health.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and cosmetic dental bonding.

Laser Dentistry

Advanced laser treatments for painless gum reshaping, decay removal, and quick healing with minimal discomfort.

Wisdom Teeth & Surgery

Safe and painless extractions, wisdom tooth removals, and minor oral surgery conducted under gentle local anesthesia.

About Our Practice

Your Comfort is Our Priority

At Ratan's Dental, we believe visiting the dentist should be a relaxing and positive experience. Our modern practice in Dhaka is designed with your absolute comfort and safety in mind.

We are equipped with advanced digital X-ray equipment, intraoral cameras, and laser dental systems to provide precise diagnostics and minimally invasive treatments. Our highly qualified team stands for hand-crafted, precision dentistry you can trust.

  • Advanced sterilization and hygiene protocols
  • 100% painless local anesthesia options
  • Friendly, English-speaking specialist doctors
